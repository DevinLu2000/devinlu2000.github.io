About me

Howdy there internet * explorer *! Now that you've stumbled upon my page, let me introduce myself! I am a recent computer science graduate from Simon Fraser University.

I love to program (obviously) and combine my passion for coding with all of my various different interests! At the moment, my current goal is to pursue a career in software development, but I am also on a side hustle and learning data science and machine learning on the side.

Apart from career goals, I am an avid (and somewhat competitive) chess player with aspirations of pursuing a GM title in chess. So far, the highest level I played was in a provincial tournament back in high school. I also have been beatboxing for around 7 years now, and have competed in the Canadian Champs in 2019. Additionally, I love to lift weights in my spare time, and train in MMA, with aspirations of competing in MMA and obtaining a black belt in jiu-jitsu. As for relaxing, I like to play video games like Valorant, watch anime or TV shows, or just read manga. That's me in a nutshell!
